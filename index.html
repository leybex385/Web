About Us

Valence Medical Group is a specialized investment and advisory firm with a background spanning international business, medical technology, and cross-border capital strategy. With a foundation in Singapore and operations extending to Los Angeles and New York, we bring a global perspective shaped by deep expertise and hands-on experience in operating businesses across healthcare, beauty, and medical device distribution. Our work centers on building and scaling platforms rather than isolated ventures, combining disciplined investment thinking, operational execution, and long-term value creation across global markets.

Leadership & Board Advisory

We provide consulting services to organizations in healthcare, international business, and regulated markets, focusing on strategic positioning, cross-border execution, and long-term sustainable platform development. Our work centers on providing professional insights on market access, operational structure, and capital allocation, grounded in extensive practical experience within cross-border environments.

Our advisory approach emphasizes decision quality, risk awareness, and sustainable growth, prioritizing long-term value over short-term optimization.
